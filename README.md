## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from SNITCH Fashion Sale to identify revenue trends, customer buying behavior, product category performance, and regional sales contribution.

The primary business objective is to identify key performance indicators (KPIs) that help improve sales efficiency, optimize discount strategies, and support data-driven decision-making.

By leveraging Excel-based analysis and dashboard visualization, this project transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights.

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The following business questions were explored:

- Which region generates the highest revenue?
- Which product categories contribute most to total sales?
- How do monthly sales fluctuate over time?
- What is the relationship between discount and profit?
- Which segments drive maximum revenue?

### Dashboard Features:

- Region-wise Revenue Analysis  
- Category & Sub-Category Performance  
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Profit Analysis  
- Interactive Slicers (Region, Category, Time Period)  

![image_alt](

### Key Insights

- The West region generates the highest overall revenue compared to other regions.
- Clothing and premium product categories contribute the largest share of total sales.
- Monthly sales trends indicate seasonal peaks during festive and promotional periods.
- Higher discount percentages increase sales volume but reduce overall profit margins.
- A small group of repeat customers significantly contributes to total revenue.
  
### Recommendations

- Increase marketing campaigns in high-performing regions to maximize revenue growth.
- Optimize discount strategies to balance customer demand and profitability.
- Maintain sufficient inventory for top-selling categories to avoid stock-outs.
- Launch loyalty programs to retain repeat customers.
- Use seasonal sales patterns for demand forecasting and inventory planning.
